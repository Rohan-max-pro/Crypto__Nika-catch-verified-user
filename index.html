About Billions Network

Billions Network is a next generation digital infrastructure designed to verify real humans online without collecting biometrics, without centralized databases and without compromising personal privacy.

In a world increasingly dominated by bots, fake accounts, deepfakes, and AI generated identities, Billions Network introduces a new standard: device level proof of humanity.

Introduces KYA - Know Your Agent.

Our Co-founder @provenauthority and team of @billions_ntwk Discovers the new concept of filtering the fake agent in the internet and increasing the trust of real agent. What more important you can decide on your own which agent is good or bad.
